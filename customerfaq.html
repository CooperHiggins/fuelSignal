About

What is FuelSignal?

The FuelSignal app serves as a rewards hub where users can access coupons, see mileage based fuel rewards, digitize loyalty cards and receive location-based promotional offers. FuelSignal is a digital way to rack up store points, Fuel Credits, and redeem the fuel reimbursement rewards and points at participating fuel retailers.

FuelSignal rewards you with free gas money for driving and making purchases at participating retailers. It is the industry's first loyalty program that rewards customers with free fuel money for every drive they make to FuelSignal retailers.

How Does FuelSignal Work?

Customers see all the FuelSignal retailers’ coupons, promotions, and deals from nearby restaurants, beauty salons, ice cream shops, grocery stores, and so much more. Next, customers select the promotion from retailers by simply tapping the promotion. You may see an offer such as:
 (i.e. Pizza shop promotion: Take $4 off a $10 regular priced lunch, plus get an additional .50 When you visit the Pizza shop, you will simply present your saved coupon offer at checkout, saving you $4.00 on lunch. Once the transaction is completed, you will see the additional 50 cents of Fuel Credit in your account that you may now spend at any FuelSignal gas station. All FuelSignal gas stations are also offering great in-store promotions and additional discounts on all fuel purchases. How Do I Become A FuelSignal Member?

Takes less than 60 seconds, all you have to do is download the free FuelSignal app and sign-up. In some cases you may also be able to sign-up at a business directly with an employee.
