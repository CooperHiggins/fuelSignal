Learn how Mary is able to save money with FuelSignal


Here is how Mary is saving money with FuelSignal.

Mary finds the deals she likes and “Taps” to get free gas money when she uses the merchants promotion.

Later that morning...

Mary went shopping for clothes at the mall. She redeems the promotions from her phone and saved $10. Mary also received $.75 cents in free gas money from the retailer for being a loyal customer.

Around lunchtime,

Mary found another deal from a popular local burger house that was 3 miles away and “Tapped” to save it. She was able to save $2 on her burger and also being a loyal customer she was rewarded with $.50 cents in free gas.

Later That Day

Mary went to her favorite coffee shop and saved money on her latte and croissant. She was also able to earn $.35 cents in free gas money.

On Her Way Home

Mary’s “low fuel alert” light turned on and she used the FuelSignal app to find the closest gas station with the best price. Mary accumulated $1.60 in free gas money today and redeems at the gas station. Now Mary is happy because she saved money on her shopping, lunch, coffee, and gas all with FuelSignal.
