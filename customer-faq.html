Customer Frequently Asked Questions

About

What is FuelSignal?

The FuelSignal app makes it easy for its users to save money on fuel by shopping at participating retailers and gas stations. The FuelSignal Loyalty Program mobile app solution help businesses and communities connect in real-time and convert every customer transaction into a relationship. FuelSignal offers a unique proposition in the industry that is attractive to consumers and to businesses. FuelSignal is the industry's first "Connected Car" based Fuel Reimbursement Loyalty Program platform for small and large fuel and non-fuel retailers.

How does FuelSignal work?

Customers see all the FuelSignal retailers’ coupons, promotions, and deals from nearby restaurants, beauty salons, ice cream shops, grocery stores, and so much more. Next, customers select the promotion from retailers by simply tapping the promotion. You may see an offer such as:

  • i.e. Pizza shop promotion: Take $4 off a $10 regular priced lunch, plus get an additional .50 cents in Fuel Credit)

When you visit the Pizza shop, you will simply present your saved coupon offer at checkout, saving you $4.00 on lunch. Once the transaction is completed, you will see the additional 50 cents of Fuel Credit in your account that you may now spend at any FuelSignal gas station.

All FuelSignal gas stations are also offering great in-store promotions and additional discounts on all fuel purchases. Thus, one single FuelSignal GPS based mobile loyalty app can save you money across many retailers.

How do I become a FuelSignal member?

Takes less than 60 seconds, all you have to do is download the free FuelSignal app and sign-up. In some cases you may also be able to sign-up at a business directly with an employee.

Where do I download FuelSignal App?

You can download FuelSignal for free through the Apple App Store and the Google Play Store.

How can FuelSignal help me save money?

FuelSignal will help you save money on all your everyday purchases at and away from the gas station. By using FuelSignal you can feel confident in knowing that you not only save money every time your car needs fuel but also at all the participating non- fuel retailers in your city. By using great promotions at partner retailers you also get free Fuel Credit, helping you save on those cumbersome gas prices.

How much is FuelSignal?

FuelSignal membership is completely, absolutely, without a doubt free for all customers. The more you use FuelSignal, the more money you will save and earn.

Where can I use FuelSignal?

Tap the “Near Me” button on the home screen and you will see a list of everything nearby such as restaurants, salons, gas stations and so much more. You can sort the results by price or promotional discount being offered. FuelSignal encompasses a large network of retailers such as salons, restaurants, coffee shops, our extensive gas station network, and so much more.

How do I earn and redeem Fuel Credit?

With FuelSignal you earn free gas money for using coupons and promotions at all of our partner stores. Use the FuelSignal app to have your “tap n’ save” QR code scanned before you make any purchase to make sure you earn rewards. If you are redeeming promotions you will either need to have the QR code within the specific promotion scanned or if you’re using more than 1, mark all the deals you want to use and they will be saved into your “tap n’ save” QR code to be redeemed in 1 scan.

How do I know the gas prices are correct?

Our prices will always be accurate and we do this by getting prices directly from our partner stores. Because we work directly with our stores all promotions, discounts, and gas prices will always be accurate.

How can I find the closest gas station to me?

Tap the “Gas Stations Near Me” button from the home screen to see a list of the results. FuelSignal uses your GPS location to give you the most accurate search results.

Why should I turn on my GPS?

Turning on your GPS will help you in several ways.

  1. When you click “gas stations near me” you will be given accurate store and travel distances.
  2. You will be able to track your exact mileage
  3. Your Fuel Log purchase history will be accurate
  4. Your “Low fuel alert” will help you when you most need it

What is the “low fuel alert” notification and how does it work?

The “low fuel alert “ is a notification you receive when you are running low on fuel. From the “Settings” section of the app you will click “Update Vehicle” and go to the last field titled “Alert at Minimum Fuel Level”. This is where you choose how many miles away from empty you would like to receive the “low fuel alert”.

  • i.e. You set your alert to 20 miles before empty. When you car is 20 miles away from running out of gas, you will receive the “low fuel alert”

Can I use both a credit card loyalty program and FuelSignal?

Yes, even when you are using a credit card based loyalty program, you will be able to use FuelSignal to save even more on all your gas station purchases. (Many of the gas brands have credit cards, helping you save money so using FuelSignal in addition to these cards will help you save even more)

Rewards

How do I use promotions inside the store?

Using 1 promotion:

  • Simply pickup your items inside the store
  • Scan the QR code found within the promotion itself
  • Congrats, you have just redeemed the promotion and will be rewarded shortly!

Using 2 or more promotions:

  • Mark and choose all the promotions you would like to redeem for the current store visit
  • Pickup all your items inside the store
  • Scan your “tap n’ save” QR code with the cashier before you make your purchase
  • You have now just redeemed all your promotions with 1 scan

How do I earn rewards for my purchases inside the store?

  1. Within the app, choose all the promotions you would like to use and save them – will be added to your “tap n’ save” QR code
  2. Scan your “tap n’ save” FuelSignal QR code with the cashier before making a purchase
  3. Scan your “tap n’ save” FuelSignal QR code with the cashier before making a purchase
  4. The cashier will validate your receipt
  5. You get additional credit from in-store purchases (some stores offer some % back to customers)

*If only using 1 promotion inside the store, you will only need to have that 1 specific promotions QR code scanned to receive credit from the cashier.

How do I earn rewards for my gas purchases?

  1. Within the app, choose all the promotions you would like to use and save them – will be added to your “tap n’ save” QR code
  2. Scan your “tap n’ save” FuelSignal QR code with the cashier before making a purchase
  3. All promotions are now redeemed
  4. The cashier will validate your receipt
  5. You get additional credit from in-store purchases (some stores offer some % back to customers)

*If only using 1 promotion inside the store, you will only need to have that 1 specific promotions QR code scanned to receive credit from the cashier.

How do I earn rewards for my gas purchases?

  1. Inside the store you will scan your “tap n’ save” FuelSignal QR code.
  2. The cashier will validate your receipt
  3. You get credit for each gallon of fuel you purchase

How can I redeem my rewards at gas stations?

Scan your “tap n’ save” FuelSignal QR code before you buy anything and if you have any credit balance the cashier will ask you if you would like to redeem your earned credits.

*Some retailers may not be using a merchant app to scan your QR code. For theses merchants you will need to enter your FuelSignal ID into the PIN pad inside the store or outside at the pump. (FuelSignal ID is 11+phone number including area code)

How does SIGNAL CREDIT work?

You can earn SIGNAL CREDIT by going to and redeeming deals at any business that is not a gas station. FuelSignal is rewarding you with free Gas Money for going to businesses and redeeming promotions. The further you are from a business (up to 5 miles) the more Gas Money you will earn.

Where can I find my credits?

You can find your credits in the My Rewards tab within the FuelSignal app.

When can I use my discounts?

Store specific rewards can be redeemed on your next in-store or fuel purchase. All you have to do is scan your “tap n’ save” FuelSignal QR code before your purchase to see if you have credits that are ready to redeem.

Where can I use money and SIGNAL CREDITS?

Fuel Credit you earn from fueling up at FuelSignal gas stations can only be used at that specific store brand (i.e. Star Stop in Austin). SIGNAL CREDITS you earn from redeeming promotions can be used at any FuelSignal gas station location.

Can I use SIGNAL CREDITS on in-store purchases?

SIGNAL CREDIT can be used for anything at any FuelSignal gas station.

Do any of my rewards or credits expire?

Your rewards and credits do expire but you will have more than enough time to make sure you get to take advantage of your earnings. Other deals on the other hand have a limited feature time, so take advantage of them quickly.

How do I keep track of my savings and Rewards Points?

You can keep track of your Reward Points by logging in to the FuelSignal App. Simply select the store tab where you earned the points and you will see My Loyalty Rewards. Here you will see the reward credits that you have earned with that particular retailer brand.

How do I save money at the pump?

FuelSignal rewards you for your loyalty at our partner gas stations. When you pump fuel make sure to scan your “tap n’ save” QR code and have your receipt validated by the cashier, doing this will ensure that you are rewarded for all your gasoline purchases.

  1. Inside the store you will have your “tap n’ save” FuelSignal QR code scanned by the cashier
  2. The cashier will validate your receipt
  3. You get credit for each gallon of fuel you purchase

I went to a FuelSignal gas station and the pump didn’t ask for my FuelSignal ID, how can I get my credit?

Make sure to go inside the store to scan your “tap n’ save” QR code and have your receipt validated by the cashier, doing this will ensure that you are rewarded for all your gasoline purchases.

Can I share deals with my friends?

Yes, let everyone know about the app and you will also be rewarded. We have social buttons so you can share deals easily with your friends.

I don’t have a smart phone/ I don’t have my smart phone on me, how can I take advantage of deals?

We will soon launch an online sign-in option on FuelSignal.com. This way you can still earn FUEL CREDIT and take advantage of great deals!

Do I need a loyalty reward card at the gas pump?

We think you have too many cards so we don’t make you carry one either. FuelSignal does not currently use loyalty reward cards.

Account

How do I sign up for FuelSignal?

You can start saving money in less than 60 seconds. Simply, download the app, create your account, and never pay full price at gas stations ever again.

I don’t have a smart phone, can I use FuelSignal?

FuelSignal will soon release an online sign-in option for those without smart phones or prefer managing their account online. Email us any suggestions at support@fuelsignal.com.

I forgot my password, how can I login?

Within the FuelSignal app home screen simply tap ”Forgot Password” and you will soon be sent an email with the password-reset instructions.

How do I update my personal information?

Login to the FuelSignal App and tap “Settings”. Then tap “Edit Profile” to edit your name, phone number, email, password, and zip code.

How do I update information about my vehicle?

Login to the FuelSignal App, tap the settings button, and then tap Update Vehicle to edit your vehicle. You will be able to edit items such as your Starting Odometer Reading, Remaining Fuel in Take, Average Mile Per Gallon, and Alert at Minimum Fuel Level.

How do I delete my account?

If you are certain that you do not want to keep saving money at partner gas stations and would like to delete your FuelSignal account email us at info@fuelsignal.com.

Who do I contact if I have an issue or want more information?

For any questions or issues email us at support@fuelsignal.com and a FuelSignal representative will contact you to resolve any issue in an efficient manner
